About Me

Hello! I’m Md. Jalilur Rahman Murtuza, a passionate Senior Software Engineer with over 8 years of experience in building innovative, high-performance software solutions. I specialize in Android and iOS app development, VoIP systems, backend platform engineering, and telecommunication and hospitality technologies.

Currently, I work at Bengal Mobile QA Solution, as part of the Opsor team on Jazzware. I’m involved in developing RT-1000, a next-generation Edge Enterprise Service Bus (ESB) that integrates PBX, PMS, Voicemail, and keycard control systems—designed to streamline and automate operations across the hospitality industry.
